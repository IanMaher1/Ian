Our Story


In the 2018/2019 term The Institute of Technology Blanchardstown is currently experiencing some historic changes when it comes to our identity and development.
We are currently preparing to unite with The Dublin Institute of Technology and the Institute of Technology Tallaght into what will become
known as Technological University Dublin or ‘TU Dublin’. As this is such a monumental time in ITB’s history, it is essential that
we take action to document the story of ITB and how what we have done, are doing and will do can shape our future as a Technological University.
As a result, The Institute of Technology Blanchardstown CDM year 2 group have been asked to produce
a ‘Story of ITB’ website along with a scope document, entailing the development of our websites as part of our Website Design module in Semester 1.
The aim of this website is to provide information on the past, present and future of ITB, its staff, student and campus.
This will be achieved from a combination of historical media (photos, documents etc) and our own primary and secondary research, as well as some original content.






The alliance of Dublin Institute of Technology, Institute of Technology Blanchardstown and Institute of Technology Tallaght is set to become the
first Technological University (TU Dublin) in Ireland. The University will be located on three campuses at Grangegorman, Blanchardstown
and Tallaght creating an inclusive and open learning experience offering pathways to graduation from apprenticeship to PhD level to a diverse range of students.
